About Me

Joseph Stewart "Yung Stew"

Joseph Steward, better known by his stage name, YungStew Music - is a Trinidadian-American rapper, singer, songwriter, musician, and entreprenuer. Born in Naperville, IL. and raised in Orlando, Fl. He gained a love for music almost instantly. Growing up singing and playing trumpet in church, he was drawn to a plethora of diverse music genres early on.

In high school, Joseph met a group of like-minded musicians with the same eagerness for music and the experience tat came with it. Starting off playing in concerts and jam sessions together, the group built chemistry quickly. They came together to be The Ignorant Skwad Krew, or Tisk, a music group that would shake hte industry for years to come. Thus YungStew Music, aka YSM was born.
